# dev.to community & ethics

- Credit sources and include canonical links; avoid plagiarism and AI-generated filler; be transparent about assumptions.
- Be inclusive: avoid slang/idioms that exclude readers; provide examples for multiple platforms/frameworks when relevant.
- Moderate comments constructively; correct errors quickly; update posts with changelogs when behavior changes.
- Respect licensing of code samples; prefer MIT/Apache snippets; attribute authors; avoid embedding secrets.
- Encourage learning paths and safe experimentation; warn about production risks and provide rollback guidance.
